Welcome To TopNotchFitness Training Gym

TopNotchFitness is just the right training gym for anyone.
We love to help our customers, reach their goals by giving the best support possible.

TopNotchFitness was founded in 2005 by Eugene Simmons who started off at a solid 240 lbs.
He always thought that he could do much better at keeping his health intact but he just needed the right motivation.

As he went to try and find the right way for him to lose weight and gain muscle as well, he thought everyone loves a good gym but you also need a trainer.
This created the idea for TopNotchFitness. He wanted to help others achieve what he knew was a common goal in life, which he considered weight loss. Eugene then took the neccersary steps to turn his vision into a reality. Today there are several TopNotchFitness gyms all across the country. There have also been rumors of them going international!
